Animal Shelter

You have been hired by a local animal shelter to develop an online ordering system for future pet owners. The animal shelter doesn't let future pet owners choose specific pets, they can only choose the type of pet being: Dog, Cat, Rabbit, Fox. You must show all of the available pet types and give future pet owners a way to add them to the cart. Show the future pet owners their cart and allow them to remove the last pet they added.
